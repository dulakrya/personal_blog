Ryan Dulak is a senior at Michigan State University studying Information Science. When Ryan was six months old he and his family moved to Rockford Michigan where he spent most of his childhood. While growing up, every summer since Ryan was eight years he would go backpacking, and he has traveled to;

  • Isle Royale National Park
  • Procupine Mountains
  • Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore
  • Pukaskwa National Park
  • The Boundry Waters Canoe Area Wilderness

Ryan's first job was being a lifeguard at an outdoor pool during summers, then while he was a freshman got a job with the MSU Residential and Hospitality Services IT department. For the past 3 years he has risen to become the most senior student and the student training supervisor for the IT department. Once he graduates he hopes to continue in the IT field and move out of Michigan to explore other states.
